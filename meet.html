Meet the Artist

Meet Spaghetti Bird!


They are a teenage artist from Denver, Colorado.
They were born with a mind for creativity and a paintbrush in their hand.

They enjoy abstract art the most because it has no limits or rules.
Their favorite medium is paint or digital drawing.

Their artwork has been featured at the Lone Tree Arts Center and The Curtis Center for the Arts.
They have also participated in assisting with set design artwork for their school musical.
